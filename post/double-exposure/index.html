    Double exposure on film photography

    When doing multiple exposures, measure the scene for the total exposure time. Then, for a double exposure, shoot at half that exposure time on each shot, i.e., for a 1 second exposure, half-a-second on each shot. For a 3-exposure, divide by 3, and so on.
