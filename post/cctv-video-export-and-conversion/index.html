CCTV Video Export and Conversion

In Smart PSS, select Playback.

Select CAM1 or CAM2, then the date and time. Select 1 hour maximum. Press play. Press scissors’ icon. Click towards the end of the timespan and press the scissors icon again. Confirm the download, by selecting the format - original format (DAV).

Rename file with a shorter file name.

To convert to a MP4 file:

ffmpeg -i video_file.dav video_file.mp4

To convert to a MP4 file, up to 16 times faster:

ffmpeg -i video_file.dav -filter:v "setpts=PTS/16" video_file.mp4
